Key Word:young migrant workers, urban-rural inequality, social capital， embeddedness and detachment，social network.

Welcome

Abstract

    With the young migrant workers in Beijing as the object of study, this thesis examines why their living status are laborious but enriched through participant observation and interviews. Moreover, in the perspective of capital, this thesis investigates the method and process that they make use of typical resources to convert their culture and social capital into the economic capital.
    Based on the perspective of embeddedness, this thesis also leverages the concept of social network to investigate current situation of young migrant workers under a view of deintercalation and embeddability in network.
